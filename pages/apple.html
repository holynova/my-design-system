01

Design Philosophy

Do

Clarity: Text should be extremely legible at all sizes, dynamic type enabled.

Deference: Minimal framing so that product photos and specifications stand out.

Depth: Use frosted overlays and clean elevation scales to map contexts.

Don't

No solid black: Use slate-black `#1d1d1f` for high legibility reading blocks.

No heavy card shadows: Cards are defined by borders or light surface contrast.

No pure decorative color blocks: Color communicates meaning, not decoration.
