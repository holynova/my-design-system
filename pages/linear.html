Design Philosophy

Linear Is

Dark-mode first: optimized deep backgrounds with high-contrast text layers.

Calm UI: neutral spaces with low noise and very subtle borders.

Glow & Focus: signature neon purple accents directing user task flows.

Structured Bento: unified grids holding dense components cleanly.

Linear Is Not

No plain grays: gray layers are space-navy tinted to maintain a premium feel.

No round elements: card corners lock strictly at 12px for a precise structure.

No slow animations: transitions are snappy (<300ms) to feel highly responsive.
